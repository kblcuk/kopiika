Privacy Policy for Kopiika

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Kopiika (the app) is an offline-first personal finance app.

Summary

  • We do not require account creation.
  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
  • Your financial data is stored locally on your device.

Information We Process

Kopiika processes data that you enter, such as:

  • entities (for example: categories, buckets)
  • plans
  • transactions
  • market value snapshots for investment accounts
  • settings (for example: reminder, period, or display preferences)

This data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device.

Data Sharing

We do not transmit your financial records to our servers because we do not operate a backend for this app.

Data may leave your device only when you explicitly choose to use platform features such as sharing, importing, or exporting files.

If you enable transaction reminders, Kopiika schedules local notifications on your device. Notification content may include transaction details such as entity names and amounts, and is handled by your device operating system.

Third-Party Services

Kopiika is built with Expo/React Native libraries. We do not intentionally integrate advertising, user-tracking, or behavior analytics services in the app.

Platform providers (for example, Google Play) may collect technical data under their own policies.

Children

Kopiika is not directed to children under 13.

Security

Your data remains primarily on your device. You are responsible for device-level security controls, such as passcode/biometric lock and backups.

Your Choices

You can delete app data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage in your device settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The Last updated date at the top reflects the current version.
